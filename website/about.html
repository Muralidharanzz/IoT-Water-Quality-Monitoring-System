What is AquaSense?

AquaSense is a real-time IoT water quality monitoring system that uses ESP32 microcontrollers and sensors to continuously measure critical water parameters. The data is transmitted to Firebase and displayed on a beautiful, intelligent dashboard accessible from any device.

Our system provides intelligent disease prediction, safety classification, and risk assessment — going beyond simple monitoring to protect your community's health.

Parameters Monitored

pH Level

Measures acidity/alkalinity of water

Safe: 6.5 – 8.5
TDS (Total Dissolved Solids)

Total mineral content in water

Safe: < 300 ppm
Turbidity

Cloudiness caused by suspended particles

Safe: < 5 NTU
Temperature

Water temperature affecting bacterial growth

Safe: < 35°C

Water Intelligence Engine

Our intelligent engine analyzes all parameters simultaneously to provide:

Exceeded Status Recommendation
0 Safe to Drink Water is safe for drinking and cooking
1 Slightly Contaminated Boil before drinking
2 Moderately Unsafe Use purifier before consumption
3 Highly Unsafe Avoid drinking, use only for cleaning
4 Severe Contamination Do not use water, immediate action required

FAQ

ESP32 sensors measure water parameters (pH, TDS, Turbidity, Temperature) and send data to Firebase Realtime Database. The web dashboard fetches this data in real-time and runs our Water Intelligence Engine to classify quality, predict diseases, and assess risk levels.

ESP32 microcontroller, pH sensor module, TDS sensor, turbidity sensor, DS18B20 temperature sensor, and a Wi-Fi connection for cloud data transmission.

Community users can register and view water quality data after admin approval. Admins have full access including user management, data export, and historical analysis.

Disease prediction is based on WHO and BIS guidelines for water quality parameters. It identifies potential risks when multiple parameters exceed safe limits simultaneously. It serves as an early warning system — always consult medical professionals for health concerns.
